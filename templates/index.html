What this page is about

Greetings Traveler !
Stay a while and listen:

The goal of this Project is to provide you with an easy interface for playing and creating Textadventures you can share with your friends.
Maybe you rember those "if you choose to X go to page 45" Books ?
Thats pretty much what we aim to provide you with.
If you want you can hop in and try our demo adventure right now !

For the creative among you we try to make creating adventures as easy and as fun as possbile so you don't get distracted by the interface and can focus on telling your story !
To achive this we offer you a texteditor with live preview, easy text formating that doesn't require you to press buttons the whole time if you don't want to. Furthermore drag and drop support for linking your story locations together !.


Once your adventure gets bigger and it starts getting hard to keep track of all the ways a player can take you'll get a graphical represantation of the ways through your adventure enabling you to find loose ends.

The graph will show you loose ends and unreachable stations in your adventure while also highlightning endpoints you defined as 'Adventure won here' or 'Adventure lost here' makeing it, hopefully, as easy as possbile for you to keep track of everything you build.

For the future we aim to provide ways to import and export the games into different formats and as standalone Versions you could, for example, embed into your own website. But also we will try to extend the possibilities for adventure creation, think conditions like Player found the red key ? and a zoomable scrollable adventure overview map.

If you want to get in touch with us please visit the 'About us' page

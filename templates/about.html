About txtadv

txtadv is project that was born as an idea a few years ago. We took that chance of participating in the djangodash 2010. This is a programming contest that gives you 48 hours to develop a new website from scratch.

The cool thing for you -- at least if you are also a programmer -- is that the code is available on github. We are always very happy about any comments related to the code and also accept patches if you are motivated to hack on the project. Get your hands dirty and tell us what you have developed.

Problems

If you experience problems with the site, like errors or any other issues -- please contact us. Our prefered way to track issues it to open a bug report on github. If you don't like github or just have no account there, feel also free to write us a mail. Use the email addresses listed below for this.

Feedback

Please feel free to give us any feedback you have about txtadv. We love to hear from you -- either bad or good critiques. Everything is welcome. We always strive to make things we've created better and to improve them over time.

A short Thank you! is ofcourse also much appreciated if you enjoy to use the site.

You can use many ways to get in touch with us. Either contact us directly with the details you can find below or use our txtadv twitter account. Write us a messages there and follow us to get all news related to this site.

About txtadv

txtadv is project that was born as an idea a few years ago. We took that chance of participating in the djangodash 2010. This is a programming contest that gives you 48 hours to develop a new website from scratch.

The cool thing for you -- at least if you are also a programmer -- is that the code is available on github. We are always very happy about any comments related to the code and also accept patches if you are motivated to hack on the project. Get your hands dirty and tell us what you have developed.
